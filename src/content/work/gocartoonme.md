What started as a high school experiment — trying to cartoon my own photo on a cheap graphics tablet — grew into something I never expected. Over 10 years later, GoCartoonMe is a hand-drawn portrait service with a YouTube community of 24,700 subscribers and 3.4 million views.

Every portrait is drawn by hand. No AI, no filters. Just craft.

## Technical side

Intentionally kept simple. Static site built with Astro and Tailwind CSS. Orders are processed through Gumroad, contact form through Formspree. Deployed on Cloudflare Pages — fast global CDN, zero cost, zero maintenance.

The right tool for the job.
